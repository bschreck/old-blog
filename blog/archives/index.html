About Me

My name is Ben Schreck, and this is my blog. I'm a junior at MIT studying electrical engineering and computer science, with plans to venture into the crazy land of music technology and change the way we interact with music. I also pole vault, and this blog chronicles how my life shapes and is shaped by my pole vault endeavours. I'll attempt to show you my adventures up till this point through odd ideas related to pole vaulting, meditation, zen buddhism, rap music, talking to girls, dealing with confidence, and contemplating life itself. Hopefully I'll leave you with a window into my mind.
